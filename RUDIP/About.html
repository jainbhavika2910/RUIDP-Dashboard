Rajasthan Uraban Infrastructure Development Project



RUIDP (Rajasthan Urban Infrastructure Development Project) is an initiative by the Government of Rajasthan, funder of RUIDP is an international agencies like the Asian Development Bank (ADB), to improve urban infrastructure and services in various cities across the state.



Main Objectives of RUIDP:


1. Improve Basic Urban Services – such as water supply, sewerage, drainage, roads, and solid waste management.

2.Enhance Public Health – by reducing pollution, improving sanitation, and ensuring clean drinking water.

3.Promote Sustainable Development – through integrated urban planning and infrastructure.

4.Strengthen Urban Governance – by building capacity in local municipal bodies and introducing reforms.

About Us

RUIDP (Rajasthan Urban Infrastructure Development Project), backed by ADB funding, targets enhancement of urban services across key cities, including Jodhpur .In Jodhpur, the focus spans water supply, sewerage, drainage, roads, and solid waste management to improve living conditions, boost public health, and support environmental resilience .The Rajasthan Urban Infrastructure Development Project (RUIDP) encompasses a comprehensive approach to modernizing urban infrastructure across Rajasthan’s cities. One of its major components is Water Supply Projects, which include laying new pipelines, installing pumps, and constructing reservoirs to ensure a continuous and reliable 24x7 water supply in selected urban zones. Another crucial area is Sewerage and Drainage Systems, which involve expanding sewer networks, building Sewage Treatment Plants (STPs), and implementing stormwater drainage solutions to prevent urban flooding during the monsoon season. Under Roads and Urban Transport, the project undertakes road widening, construction of flyovers, and junction improvements to enhance connectivity between residential and commercial areas, reducing congestion and improving mobility.Finally, the project emphasizes Institutional Reforms and Capacity Building by offering training programs for Urban Local Bodies (ULBs) and supporting systems for e-governance, financial management, and improved service delivery. Together, these integrated efforts aim to create cleaner, more efficient, and resilient urban environments across Rajasthan.

Our Projects

SEWERAGE PROJECT

The sewerage project under RUIDP in Jodhpur is a key initiative aimed at improving the city's sanitation infrastructure and urban environment.One of the notable developments includes the construction and upgrading of Sewage Treatment Plants (STPs) to treat wastewater before its safe disposal or reuse.This initiative plays a vital role in making Jodhpur a cleaner, healthier, and more sustainable urban center under the RUIDP framework.

DRAINAGE PROJECT

The drainage project under RUIDP in Jodhpur focuses on addressing the city's long-standing problems of waterlogging and urban flooding, particularly during the monsoon season. As part of this initiative, a well-planned stormwater drainage system has been developed to efficiently collect and redirect rainwater from roads, residential colonies, and public spaces to natural outfalls or storage areas.
